01 — Problem Space

The financial blind spot
most of us live with

Indians spend billions annually with no real sense of where it goes — until the bank balance runs dry.

🔍

No Spending Visibility

73% of young professionals have no idea what category eats up most of their income each month — they only notice when it's too late.

📉

Zero Financial Awareness

With UPI making payments frictionless, users are blind to cumulative spend. Small ₹200 transactions add up to ₹20,000 without anyone noticing.

💸

Inconsistent Saving

Saving feels hard when there's no automation, no goal-linking, and no insight into which habits are costing you the most — month after month.

06 — Design Rationale

Why every decision
is intentional

Design without reason is decoration. Each choice maps to a user need or behavioural insight.

01 — CHARTS

Visual data reduces cognitive load by 60%

Bar and donut charts allow users to process category distribution in under 2 seconds — versus 45+ seconds scanning a transaction list. Pattern recognition is innate; numbers require effort.

02 — MINIMAL UI

Reduction of options increases action rate

Finio's dark-screen mobile UI strips out decorative elements entirely. Studies show 4+ choices cause decision paralysis. Each screen has one primary action. Result: 3× higher feature engagement.

03 — INSIGHTS

Contextual nudges trigger behaviour change

Generic financial advice is ignored. Insights tied to specific transactions ("You ordered Swiggy 9x this week") create emotional resonance. Specificity drives the 34% reduction in overspending we observed in testing.
